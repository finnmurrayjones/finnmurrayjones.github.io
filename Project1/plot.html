PLOT SYNOPSIS
This film takes you on a journey, and follows the adventures of four young students moving into an international university somewhere on a desert island in the Arabian Gulf. Follow Antony, Jiji, Baleegh and Ahmad as they discover a whole new world under their feet, and their bravery and camraderie develop as they adapt to the environment around them. In order to stay alive they must work together, but how long can it last? Watch the film to find out how this story ends...
